## 2. Core Definitions (From Manifesto)

- **For-Good Businesses**: Enterprises that prioritize positive externalities (community health, worker dignity, environmental restoration) over short-term profit maximization.
- **Conviction-Driven Founders**: People who decided on an outcome first and built a business as the means to achieve it. The business is the vehicle; the conviction is the engine.
- **The Acumen Gap**: For-good founders are often brilliant at their craft but lack the resources to prove it to a cynical market trained by greenwashing.
- **The Living Signal**: A BFG grade that is not a one-time certification, but renewed annually and informed by ongoing consumer feedback.
- **The 30% Goal**: Building toward a Malaysia where for-good businesses account for 30% of national GDP.

### Data Model
- **`businesses` collection**: Core business profiles. Key fields: `score` (map `{s, e, c, soc, env}`), `lastAuditDate`, `membershipType` (`full` | `affiliate`), `purposeStatement`, `branches[]`, `stewardship.managers[]`, `stewardship.crew[]`.
- **`audit_logs` collection**: Now includes `scores` (map), `pillarCommentary` (map), `verificationChecklist` (map) — populated by the refactored AuditHub.
- **`feedback` collection**: Consumer observations and recommendations. Type: `'Recommendation'` | `'Observation'`.
- **`public_activity` collection**: Sanitized, anonymized network activity for the Newsreel. **Periodically populated by a server-side cloud function** (not real-time, by design, to control costs).
- **`system/stats` document**: Reconciled global stats (consumers, businesses, checkins, purchases, gdpPenetration, etc.). Updated every 5 minutes by a server-side cron.
- **`users/{uid}/seasons/{seasonId}`**: Seasonal personal stats. `seasonId` generated by `getSeasonId()` in `badgeLogic.js`.
- **`announcements` collection**: Platform-wide or targeted messages for the Newsreel.
- **`transactions` collection**: Individual check-ins, purchases, rewards.
- **`applications` collection**: Business membership applications.

### Newsreel Architecture (Pseudo-Real-Time Pattern)
The Newsreel combines two streams to create the *perception* of a live network:
1. **Server-side** (`public_activity`): Curated, anonymized snapshot populated periodically by cloud function. Read cold, low-cost.
2. **Client-side** (`addLocalActivity`): Instant, ephemeral injection of the *current user's* own actions. Disappears on refresh.

This gives real-time feedback for the user's own actions, while the broader network activity refreshes periodically. **This is working correctly and is architecturally complete.** Do not propose changes to this pattern.

### Badge/Tier System
The manifesto references "Blue/Silver/Gold/Platinum" tiers. The app uses "Scout/Steward/Guardian/Legend" — this is an **intentional evolution**, documented via JSDoc in `badgeLogic.js`. Do not flag this as a discrepancy.

## 4. The Three Horizons Framework

### Horizon 1 — Emotional Micro-Interactions ✅ COMPLETE
The platform has:
- A Milestone Gallery with animations (check-in/purchase milestones).
- A MilestoneCelebration component for unlocked milestone replays.
- Movement-driven copy throughout the scanner flow ("You just chose conviction over convenience. That matters.").
- A SeasonalSnapshot component for personal seasonal stats.

**Status**: This horizon is complete. Do not re-propose these items.

### Horizon 2 — The Discovery Layer 🔵 IN PROGRESS
The Directory currently functions like a database. The manifesto says businesses need to be *Seen*. The gap is surfacing the *why* of each business at the point of discovery, not just after clicking through.

Three specific gaps:
1. **"Why This Business" Narrative Card** — Surface `purposeStatement`, plain-language score interpretation, and recommendation count directly on Directory listing cards.
2. **Conviction-Strength Filtering** — Allow ambassadors to filter the Directory by Stakeholder Paradigm strength (e.g., "Show me businesses with A or B in the Employee paradigm").
3. **"Conviction of the Week"** — A featured business slot to surface hidden gems and drive discovery beyond the top-ranked businesses.

### Horizon 3 — Network Effect Visibility 🔵 IN PROGRESS
The infrastructure is already in place (newsreel, seasons, stats). The gap is **narrative framing** — making the user feel their individual contribution is part of the larger 30% movement.

Two specific gaps:
1. **Seasonal Reflection (Outward Framing)** — The SeasonalSnapshot exists but is inward-looking. Add context: *"Your 12 supports this season contributed to X% of the network's total activity."*
2. **The 30% Progress Story** — `gdpPenetration` is already on the Home screen as a number. Add narrative labels that change as the number moves (e.g., *"0.04% — The foundation is being laid."*).
